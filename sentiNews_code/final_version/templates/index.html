Why did we choose to make Senti-News?

We created Sentinews to:

  1. Expose bias by analyzing news articles for political and ideological leanings.
  2. Use AI to categorize content by sentiment, bias, and subject for quicker insights.
  3. Enhance media literacy by revealing hidden biases and perspectives.
  4. Provide in-depth, user-friendly analysis missing from other tools.
